15–18 Questions | Sabse Zyada Marks

1️⃣ Percentage HAR SAAL

📐 Important Formulas:
x% of y = (x × y) / 100
% Increase = [(New − Old) / Old] × 100
% Decrease = [(Old − New) / Old] × 100
A, B se x% zyada hai → B = A / (1 + x/100)
Successive % Change: a% aur b% = a + b + (ab/100)%
🔥 Shortcut Tricks:

➤ 10% of any number = Number ÷ 10
➤ 25% = ÷4 | 50% = ÷2 | 33.33% = ÷3
➤ Agar a% of X = b% of Y, toh X/Y = b/a

Q: X got 98 marks in exam which is 56% of total marks. Max marks?
A) 150
B) 175
C) 175 ✓
D) 200
Solution: 56% of Total = 98 → Total = (98 × 100) / 56 = 175
Q: Find single equivalent increase if successively increased by 20%, 25%, 30%?
A) 75%
B) 85%
C) 95% ✓
D) 35%
Solution: 1.20 × 1.25 × 1.30 = 1.95 → 95% increase

2️⃣ Profit & Loss HAR SAAL

📐 Formulas:
Profit = SP − CP
Profit% = (Profit / CP) × 100
SP = CP × (100 + P%) / 100
CP = SP × 100 / (100 + P%)
Discount = MP − SP
Discount% = (Discount / MP) × 100
When SP of 'a' articles = CP of 'b' articles → Gain% = (b−a)/a × 100
🔥 Important Trick:

Agar SP of 80 = CP of 100 → Gain% = (100−80)/80 × 100 = 25%
Agar kharida 20% kam, becha 45% fayda pe → Profit on MP = (1.45 × 0.80 − 1) × 100 = 16%

Q: On selling 17 balls at ₹840, loss = cost of 5 balls. Cost of 1 ball?
A) ₹60
B) ₹70 ✓
C) ₹80
D) ₹90
Solution: SP of 17 = CP of 12 → 840 = 12 × CP → CP = ₹70
Q: SP of 80 articles = CP of 100. Gain%?
A) 30%
B) 25% ✓
C) 40%
D) 50%
Solution: Gain% = (100−80)/80 × 100 = 25%

3️⃣ Simple & Compound Interest HAR SAAL

📐 Formulas:
SI = (P × R × T) / 100
A = P + SI = P(1 + RT/100)
CI = P[(1 + R/100)^T − 1]
CI − SI (2 year) = P(R/100)²
P = (SI × 100) / (R × T)
🔥 Smart Trick for SI:

Agar amount 5 saal mein ₹520 aur 7 saal mein ₹568 ho toh:
SI per year = (568−520)/(7−5) = 48/2 = ₹24/year
Principal = 520 − (5 × 24) = 520 − 120 = ₹400

Q: Gopal invested 1/3 at 7%, 1/4 at 8%, rest at 10%. Total SI = ₹1496. Capital?
A) ₹17,000
B) ₹17,200
C) ₹17,600 ✓
D) ₹17,400
Solution: Let P = total. SI = P/3×7/100 + P/4×8/100 + 5P/12×10/100 = P(7/300 + 8/400 + 10×5/(12×100)) = P × 8.5/100 = 1496 → P = ₹17,600

a:b = c:d → ad = bc (cross multiply)

x saal pehle → present age − x
Agar ratio a:b hai aur dono mein k jodte hain → (a+k):(b+k) = new ratio
🔥 Age Problem Trick:

Ratio 3:7 → ages = 3x, 7x
2 saal baad 5:9 → (3x+2)/(7x+2) = 5/9 → 27x+18 = 35x+10 → x = 1
Present ages = 3, 7 → After 10 yrs = 13:17

A) 15:16
B) 5:17
C) 13:17 ✓
D) 13:17
Solution: 3x+2 / 7x+2 = 5/9 → x=1. Ages=3,7. After 10 yrs: 13:17

A) 32 years ✓
B) 26 years

5️⃣ Work & Time HAR SAAL

📐 Formulas:
A ka 1 din ka kaam = 1/A
A+B milkar 1 din mein karte hain = 1/A + 1/B
Total time = 1/(1/A + 1/B) = AB/(A+B)
A, B se 2x achha → A = B/2 time mein karta
Alternate days: 2 din mein kaam = 1/A + 1/B
🔥 Super Trick:

A, B se 2x achha → Ratio A:B = 1:2 (efficiency)
A+B milkar 14 din mein → 1/A + 1/B = 1/14
A = 2x efficient, B = x → 2x + x = 3x = 1/14 → x = 1/42 → A = 2/42 = 1/21 → 21 days

Q: A is twice as good workman as B. Together they finish in 14 days. A alone?
A) 18 days
B) 7 days
C) 21 days ✓
D) 28 days
Solution: A:B efficiency = 2:1. Together 14 days. A's time = 14 × 3/2 = 21 days
Q: A & B can do work in 9 & 12 days. Alternate days starting A. Total days?
A) 36
B) 10
C) 10¼ days ✓
D) 13
Solution: 2 days work = 1/9+1/12 = 7/36. In 10 days = 5×7/36 = 35/36. Remaining 1/36. A's turn = (1/36)/(1/9) = 1/4 day. Total = 10¼

7️⃣ Geometry & Mensuration HAR SAAL

📐 Key Formulas:
Circle: Area = πr² | Circumference = 2πr
Chord length = 2√(r² − d²) where d = distance from centre
Rhombus: Area = (d1 × d2)/2 | Side = √[(d1/2)² + (d2/2)²]
Perimeter of Rhombus = 4 × side
Triangle: Exterior angle = sum of 2 non-adjacent interior angles
Cone: Volume = πr²h/3 | Doubling h → Volume doubles (100% increase)
AP: n = (Last − First)/d + 1
Q: Circle radius 13cm, chord 5cm from centre. Chord length?
A) 25 cm
B) 24 cm ✓
C) 21 cm
D) 18 cm
Solution: Half chord = √(13² − 5²) = √(169−25) = √144 = 12. Full chord = 24
Q: Rhombus diagonals 24cm & 10cm. Perimeter?
A) 62
B) 56
C) 52
D) 52 cm ✓
Solution: Side = √(12²+5²) = √(144+25) = √169 = 13. Perimeter = 4×13 = 52
Q: Area of circle numerically = circumference. Diameter?
A) 2 units
B) 4 units ✓
C) 1 unit
D) 5 units
Solution: πr² = 2πr → r = 2 → Diameter = 4
Q: AP: 3, 7, 11, ... 407. How many terms?
A) 100
B) 102 ✓
C) 99
D) 101
Solution: n = (407−3)/4 + 1 = 404/4 + 1 = 101 + 1 = 102

🔥 Mirror Image Trick:

Mirror mein 7:45 dikhta hai → 11:60 − 7:45 = 4:15 ✓

6️⃣ Syllogism HAR SAAL 1-2 Q

📌 Key Rules:
All A are B + All B are C → All A are C ✓
Some A are B + All B are C → Some A are C ✓
No A are B + Some B are C → Some C are not A ✓
"Possibility" statements: Possible agar directly contradict na ho
Complementary pairs: Either I or II follows
Q: Some names are languages. No languages are poems. Conclusions: I.No poem is name. II.All languages are names is possibility.
A) Only I
B) Only II ✓
C) Neither
D) Both
Solution: "Some names are languages, No languages are poems" → Some names are not poems (I is wrong, can't say NO poem is name). II is possible as nothing contradicts all languages being names. Answer: Only II

🔥 High Probability Questions — Maths

Expected Q1: A train covers 360km at 90km/h and then 120km at 60km/h. Average speed?
A) 75 km/h
B) 80 km/h
C) 80 km/h ✓
D) 72 km/h
Solution: T1 = 360/90 = 4hr. T2 = 120/60 = 2hr. Total D = 480, Total T = 6hr. Avg = 480/6 = 80km/h
Expected Q2: A can do work in 12 days, B in 18 days. Together, how many days?
A) 7.2 days ✓
B) 8 days
C) 10 days
D) 6.5 days
Solution: Together = (12×18)/(12+18) = 216/30 = 7.2 days
Expected Q3: A number's 1/5 increased by 4 = 1/4 diminished by 16. Find number?
A) 120
B) 115
C) 400 ✓
D) 390
Solution: x/5 + 4 = x/4 − 16 → x/4 − x/5 = 20 → x(5−4)/20 = 20 → x/20 = 20 → x = 400
Expected Q4: 132 candidates, successful:unsuccessful = 8:3. 6 more pass. New ratio?
A) 12:5
B) 17:5 ✓
C) 5:17
D) 3:19
Solution: Total=132. S/(U)=8/3 → S=96, U=36. 6 more pass: S=102, U=30. Ratio=102:30=17:5
Expected Q5: Two numbers ratio 5:8. Add 5 to each, becomes 2:3. Greater number?
A) 40
B) 48
C) 32
D) 40 ✓
Solution: (5x+5)/(8x+5) = 2/3 → 15x+15 = 16x+10 → x=5. Numbers=25,40. Greater=40

🔥 High Probability — Reasoning

Expected Q6: A is 3 yrs younger than C but 1 yr older than D. D is 1 yr older than B but 4 yrs younger than C. C = 15. B's age?
A) 13
B) 12
C) 11
D) 10 ✓
Solution: C=15. A=15−3=12. D=12−1=11. B=11−1=10
Expected Q7: Eye:Myopia :: Teeth:?
A) Pyorrhoea ✓
B) Trachoma
C) Cataract
D) Hypermetropia
Solution: Myopia eye ki disease hai. Pyorrhoea teeth/gums ki disease hai.
Expected Q8: Alpha-numeric: D4T, F9R, H20P, J43N, ?
A) L90M
B) N90N
C) L90L
D) L90L ✓
Solution: Letters: D,F,H,J,L (+2 each). Numbers: 4,9,20,43 → ×2+1 pattern: 4×2+1=9, 9×2+2=20, 20×2+3=43, 43×2+4=90. Last letters: T,R,P,N,L (−2 each). Answer: L90L
Expected Q9: 4:17 :: 7:?
A) 48
B) 50
C) 50 ✓
D) 49
Solution: 4²+1=17. 7²+1=50
